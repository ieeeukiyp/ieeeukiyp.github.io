About the symposium venue

The symposium will take place at the University of Chester's Queen's Park Campus, on the south bank of the River Dee. Now home to the university's business school, this historic site was once the Western Command headquarters during the second world war.

Getting here

The symposium venue is very accessible and less than a 5 minute walk from the city centre

By Car

The Queen's Park campus has ample parking spaces for those arriving by car. There are several other car parks (including multi-storey), in the city centre.

Get Driving Directions

By Rail

Chester railway station is a 17 minute (0.8 mile) walk from the symposium venue. There are regular buses to the city centre from outside Chester station.

Get Walking Directions

By Air

The nearest airports to Chester are Liverpool John Lennon Airport and Manchester Airport.
